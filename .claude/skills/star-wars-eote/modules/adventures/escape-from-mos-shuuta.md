# Escape from Mos Shuuta — EotE Beginner Adventure

> Adventure module for the star-wars-eote skill. The introductory adventure from the
> Edge of the Empire Beginner Game.

---

## Setting

Mos Shuuta — a small spaceport city on Tatooine, perched atop a tall rocky bluff with sheer
cliffs on all sides. Ruled by Teemo the Hutt, a local crime boss. The only way off-planet is
to steal a ship.

---

## The Situation

The PCs have run afoul of Teemo the Hutt. Each has their own reason (see Obligations). They
need to:
1. Escape Teemo's Gamorrean enforcers in the cantina
2. Find a ship to steal — the *Krayt Fang*, a YT-1300 freighter at Landing Bay Aurek
3. Acquire a replacement hypermatter reactor igniter (the ship's is broken)
4. Release the docking clamps at Spaceport Control
5. Board the *Krayt Fang*, defeat Captain Trex, and escape

## Encounter Flow

### Encounter 1 — On the Run

Cantina. PCs hide from Gamorreans. Introduces skill checks. Each PC chooses how to hide —
the choice determines which skill is tested:
- Hide in a booth → Stealth
- Convince the Twilek dancer to hide you backstage → Charm (with Boost if same species)
- Storage closet, jam the door → Mechanics
- Climb into the ceiling struts → Athletics
- Get behind the bar, pretend to work → Deceit
- Sit at the bar and act casual → Cool

Easy difficulty (1 purple) for all checks.

### Encounter 2 — A Gang of Gamorreans

Combat breaks out in the cantina. Introduces combat mechanics: initiative (fixed order
based on Enc 1 results), actions/manoeuvres, range bands, attacks, damage/soak,
Advantage/Threat spending, Boost/Setback dice.

Gamorreans are at short/medium range from far side of room. Cantina is medium range across.

### Encounter 3 — The Junk Shop

Social encounter with Vorn Tel-Ovis. PCs must acquire the HMRI. Multiple approaches:
- **Buy it:** 500 credits (Vorn has promised it to Trex, will not sell for less)
- **Negotiate price:** Opposed Negotiate vs Vorn's Negotiate. Success = 600 credits. Fail = 750.
- **Lie (represent Trex):** Opposed Deceit vs Vorn's Discipline.
- **Flatter:** Opposed Charm vs Discipline (with Setback — Vorn is ill-natured).
- **Intimidate:** Opposed Coerce vs Discipline.
- **Steal it:** Opposed Skulduggery vs Vigilance. Or use violence.
- **Befriend the R5 droid:** R5-K3 hates Vorn. If the PCs talk to it outside, it will steal
  the HMRI and bring it to them. PCs just need to distract Vorn.

Introduces opposed checks, Boost/Setback for circumstances, Despair consequences.

### Encounter 4 — Command and Control

Spaceport Control. PCs must release docking clamps. Two security droids guard the entrance.
- **Talk past the droids:** Opposed Deceit/Coerce vs droids' Discipline. Boost for plausible
  story, Setback for implausible. Each failed attempt adds cumulative Setback.
- **Find the side entrance:** Skulduggery or Computers, Average difficulty, to bypass lock.
- **Fight the droids:** Combat. If outside droids are fought, inside droids + Brynn join.
- **Inside:** Convince Brynn (opposed social vs her Discipline), slice the computers
  (Average Computers — Threat triggers alarm), or fight.

Introduces expanded initiative rules (Cool vs Vigilance, rank by successes then advantage).

### Encounter 5 — Imperial Stormtroopers

Stormtroopers (arrived on Lambda shuttle, placed at Teemo's disposal) patrol the streets.
Two groups of 3 at medium/long range. Use Minion Group rules. **Extremely dangerous** —
the GM should make clear that fleeing may be wiser than fighting.

### Encounter 6 — All Aboard

Landing Bay Aurek. The *Krayt Fang* with loading ramp deployed. 4 security droids (2 at
entrance, 2 at ramp) + Captain Trex aboard. Multiple approaches:
- **"We have a bounty to turn in":** Deceit vs droids' Discipline (Boost if offering Lowhhrick).
- **"We are here to install the HMRI":** Droids let them aboard, Trex confronts them — Deceit
  vs Trex's Discipline or fight.
- **Sneak past:** Side entrance, Average Skulduggery/Computers. Then Stealth vs droids' Vigilance.
- **Fight:** Droids alert Trex. He takes cover in the ship and fights tactically.

Trex is a **Nemesis NPC** — dangerous, with strain threshold and Critical Injuries like a PC.

### Interlude — Experience and Destiny

After securing the ship components, award 10 XP. Introduce the Destiny Pool (Force die roll).
The player may explore Mos Shuuta further or proceed to the landing bay.

### Encounter 7 — Up, Up, and Away

Aboard the *Krayt Fang*. Crew stations: Pilot, Gunner (dorsal turret), Gunner (ventral turret),
Engineer. For solo play, the player takes one station and the GM manages the rest narratively.

4 TIE fighters (2 wings of 2, Minion Groups) attack at close range. The PCs must survive
until the HMRI warms up (~6 rounds, reduced by Engineering successes). Then jump to hyperspace.

Introduces starship combat: Gunnery checks, Evasive Manoeuvres, Damage Control, starship
damage (hull trauma and system strain vs armour).

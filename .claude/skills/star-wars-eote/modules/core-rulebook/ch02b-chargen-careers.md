# Chapter II: Careers & Specialisations

> Source: *Star Wars: Edge of the Empire Core Rulebook*, pp. 53-91

## Overview

A character's **career** is permanent and defines their overall way of life. Each career grants **8 career skills**; the player chooses **4 of these** to gain one free rank in during character creation. Career skills always cost less XP to advance.

A **specialisation** is a focused area within (or outside) a career. The character's starting specialisation grants **4 additional bonus career skills** (choose 2 for one free rank each). Additional specialisations may be purchased later with XP.

## Purchasing Additional Specialisations

| Specialisation Type | XP Cost |
|---|---|
| Within current career | 10 x new number of specialisations |
| Outside current career | 10 x new number of specialisations + 10 |

*Example: A Bounty Hunter (Assassin) purchasing Gadgeteer (same career, 2nd specialisation) costs 20 XP. Purchasing Pilot (Smuggler career, 3rd specialisation) would cost 40 XP.*

The new specialisation's 4 bonus career skills become career skills for the character. However, no free ranks are gained -- only the starting specialisation grants free ranks.
